Additional Notes for Reverse Engineers:

  • Memory Allocation: Direct syscalls (Nt*/Zw* variants) are increasingly common for EDR evasion
  • Execution Triggers: SetWindowsHookEx and manual syscalls are growing in prevalence
  • Process Hollowing: Modern variants use transacted sections and NUMA-aware allocation
  • APC Injection: Early Bird APC and NtQueueApcThread are standard for process startup injection
  • Exception Handling: SetUnhandledExceptionFilter is emerging as a stealthy execution method
  • Fiber Execution: Rare but seen in advanced APT toolkits for execution flow obfuscation
